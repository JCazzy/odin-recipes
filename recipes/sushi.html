Sushi

California Roll - it's really good. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Water
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Crab meat
  • Mayo
  • Nori
  • Sesame seeds
  • Cucumber
  • Avocados

Step by Step

  1. Wash rice
  2. Mix vinegar and sugar
  3. Mix crabmeat and mayo
  4. Lay nori and add sesame seed
  5. Place cucumber and avocado
  6. Wrap nori
  7. Cut into slices
